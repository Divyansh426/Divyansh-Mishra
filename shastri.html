About Lal Bahadur Shastri

Lal Bahadur Shastri was the second Prime Minister of India and a senior leader of the Indian National Congress. He was born on 2nd October 1904 in Mughalsarai, Uttar Pradesh. Known for his leadership during the 1965 Indo-Pak war and his famous slogan 'Jai Jawan Jai Kisan', he was deeply committed to the welfare of both farmers and soldiers.

Shastri passed away under mysterious circumstances in 1966 and was awarded the Bharat Ratna posthumously for his dedication to the country.
